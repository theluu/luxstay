Top 10 Luxury Hotels

Perched on the top floors of the Tokyo Midtown complex, The Ritz-Carlton offers panoramic views of the city and Mount Fuji. With exquisite Japanese decor, an exclusive Club Lounge, and a world-class spa, it combines traditional elegance with modern luxury. Guests can enjoy Michelin-starred dining, a serene garden, and impeccable service.

Known as the “Grande Dame of the East,” The Peninsula boasts a rich history and timeless elegance. Its colonial-style architecture blends seamlessly with modern amenities. Guests can experience the luxury of a fleet of Rolls-Royce limousines, a lavish spa, and an array of fine dining options including a Michelin-starred restaurant.

Nestled in the lush landscapes of Ubud, this resort offers a unique blend of luxury and nature. The property features private villas with plunge pools, a stunning rooftop lotus pond, and a serene riverside setting. Guests can indulge in Balinese spa treatments, culinary delights, and immersive cultural experiences.

Explore the Global Luxury

With a history spanning over 140 years, Mandarin Oriental combines classic elegance with contemporary comfort. The hotel is renowned for its exceptional service, riverside location, and luxurious rooms. Guests can enjoy fine dining at world-renowned restaurants, an acclaimed spa, and cultural experiences in the heart of Bangkok.

"Global Gastronomy: Exploring Flavors Around the World"

Overlooking the iconic Sydney Harbour, The Langham offers a sophisticated retreat with Victorian-inspired decor. The hotel features spacious rooms, a renowned day spa, and a range of dining options. Its location provides easy access to the city’s attractions and stunning views of the harbour. Aman Tokyo embodies minimalist luxury with its sleek design and serene ambiance. Situated in the Otemachi Tower, the hotel offers stunning views of the city skyline and Imperial Palace Gardens. The property includes spacious rooms, an exceptional spa, and a sophisticated dining experience.

Ocated in the heart of Mayfair, The Dorchester epitomizes classic British luxury. The hotel’s elegant rooms, world-class service, and exquisite dining options, including a Michelin-starred restaurant, make it a favorite among royalty and celebrities. The Dorchester also boasts a beautiful garden and a luxurious spa.

Located on the pristine shores of Nusa Dua, the St. Regis Bali Resort offers unparalleled luxury and privacy. The resort features spacious villas with private pools, a world-class spa, and a variety of gourmet dining options. The lush gardens and beachfront setting provide a perfect tropical escape.
